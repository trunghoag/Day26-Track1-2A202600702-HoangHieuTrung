## 2. AI Differentiation Check

**AI trong san pham giai quyet van de gi cu the, khac gi so voi cach khong dung AI?**

MagicSchool dung generative AI de giam workload cua giao vien: tao lesson plan, worksheet, quiz, feedback, support language learning, adaptive 1:1 tutoring, va just-in-time support cho hoc sinh. Neu lam khong dung AI, giao vien phai tu viet hoc lieu, tu dieu chinh noi dung cho tung nhom hoc sinh, va tu tong hop data lop hoc bang spreadsheet/manual workflow. AI o day tao gia tri vi no nam truc tiep trong workflow hang ngay cua giao vien, khong chi la mot chatbot gan them.

**Neu bo chu "AI" di, startup nay con lai loi the gi?**

Van con mot so loi the: founder co domain expertise giao duc K-12, product tap trung rieng vao school workflow, adoption tu giao vien va nha truong, school/district partnerships, va trust positioning ve privacy, safety, responsible AI. Theo cong bo cua MagicSchool, den Series B san pham da co hang trieu nguoi dung hang thang, hon 10,000 school partners, gan nhu moi U.S. school district deu co educator dung san pham, va hien dien o 160 quoc gia.

**Deal nay co roi vao bay "Unclear differentiation" khong?**

Khong ro rang roi vao bay do, vi MagicSchool khong chi noi "we use AI" ma gan AI vao tac vu cu the cua giao vien va hoc sinh. Tuy nhien, rui ro van co: nhieu cong cu LLM co the tao lesson plan/quiz, nen differentiation ben vung phai den tu education-specific workflow, trust/safety, school distribution, data/product feedback loop, va kha nang chung minh learning/workload outcomes.

## 5. Lessons For Our A3 Idea

| Lesson | Implication cho Edtech AI MVP Viet Nam/SEA |
| --- | --- |
| Distribution matters | Can co pilot voi truong/lop, khong chi product demo. |
| Trust matters | Can chuan bi privacy, safety, teacher control, va responsible AI workflow. |
| AI must attach to repeated workflow | Tap trung vao lesson plan, quiz, feedback, tutoring, progress report - cac tac vu lap lai hang tuan. |
| Outcome proof beats AI hype | Can do retention, thoi gian giao vien tiet kiem, completion rate cua hoc sinh, va willingness to pay. |
