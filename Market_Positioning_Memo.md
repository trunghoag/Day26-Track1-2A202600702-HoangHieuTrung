# Market Positioning Memo

## Question

**Idea cua toi co fundable trong boi canh goi von hien tai khong, va toi can bang chung gi de chung minh dieu do?**

## Memo

Idea **Edtech AI MVP cho Viet Nam/SEA** co fundable, nhung chi fundable neu chung minh duoc 3 thu: AI tao ra outcome ro hon cach lam manual, unit economics khong bi cost LLM an het margin, va co traction that tu giao vien/truong hoc. Trong boi canh hien tai, investor khong con de bi thuyet phuc boi cau "chung toi dung AI"; ho can thay san pham giai quyet workflow dau dau, co retention, va co duong den revenue.

Thi truong Vietnam/SEA vua co tin hieu kho vua co tin hieu tot. Theo Vietnam Innovation & Private Capital Report 2025 cua Do Ventures/VPCA/BCG, Vietnam recorded **US$2.3B across 141 deals in 2024**, deal count van on dinh du capital deployed giam, smaller early-stage deals co dau hieu rebound, va AI funding tang **8x YoY**. Dieu nay nghia la tien van co, nhung selective hon: early-stage investor se uu tien startup co bang chung su dung that, unit economics ro, va market wedge cu the. Edtech AI nam trong vung co ly do de fundable vi education co pain point lon: giao vien qua tai, hoc sinh can ca nhan hoa, nha truong can cong cu an toan va co kiem soat. Nhung day cung la sector kho ban hang vi sales cycle truong hoc cham va budget han che.

MagicSchool la benchmark de so sanh. Cong ty goi duoc **US$45M Series B** vi khong chi ban "AI chatbot", ma gan AI vao workflow lap lai cua giao vien va nha truong: lesson plan, assessment, differentiation, tutoring, language support, student-facing AI, school trust/safety. Bai hoc cho idea Viet Nam/SEA la: khong nen pitch AI nhu mot tinh nang cong nghe; phai pitch nhu mot workflow product co adoption, distribution, va outcome.

## Unit Economics So Bo

Day la estimate de lap luan, can thay bang usage data that sau pilot.

| Model | Revenue assumption | Cost assumption | Contribution margin |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| School/class pilot | US$300/month cho 100 active students/teachers | LLM/API US$60, hosting/tooling US$20, support/customer success US$70 = US$150/month | US$150/month/school, about 50% |
| Per-user subscription | US$8/user/month | LLM/API + support US$2.50/user/month | US$5.50/user/month |

Mo hinh co the fundable neu contribution margin duong sau LLM cost va support cost. Neu moi truong dung nhieu nhung khong tra tien, hoac moi student request qua nhieu lam cost AI tang nhanh, margin se yeu. Vi vay pilot phai track cost/request, active users, seat utilization, va support hours.

## Cash Needs

De di den milestone tiep theo, idea can khoang **US$150K trong 12 thang**. Muc tieu cua khoan tien nay khong phai scale marketing som, ma la chung minh product-market evidence.

| Use of funds | Allocation | Ly do |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Product/engineering | 45% | Hoan thien teacher workflow, student practice, progress dashboard, analytics |
| Pilot va customer success | 25% | Onboard truong/lop, collect feedback, support giao vien |
| AI infrastructure/hosting | 15% | Model/API cost, eval pipeline, logging, reliability |
| Compliance/privacy/legal | 10% | School data privacy, safety review, policy docs |
| Contingency | 5% | Buffer cho pilot delays va integration needs |

## KPI Truoc Vong Tiep Theo

| KPI | Target | Ly do |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Paying pilots | 5-10 school/class pilots | Chung minh willingness to pay va go-to-market |
| Active users | 1,000+ active learners/teachers | Chung minh usage volume du de hoc product behavior |
| Weekly teacher retention | >40% | Chung minh tool lap lai trong workflow that |
| Repeated weekly use cases | 3-5 use cases | Vi du: lesson plan, quiz, feedback, tutoring, progress report |
| Contribution margin | Duong sau LLM/API cost | Chung minh model khong bi AI cost pha margin |
| Case studies | 2-3 validated stories | Chung minh thoi gian giao vien tiet kiem hoac hoc sinh hoan thanh bai tot hon |
| Safety/quality | Teacher review loop + content safety rubric | Giam rui ro adoption trong school environment |

## Positioning

Nen position idea nhu **"AI workflow layer for teachers and learners in Vietnam/SEA"**, khong phai chi la "AI tutor". Wedge ban dau nen la teacher productivity + personalized practice, vi teacher la nguoi co pain point ro va co the keo student adoption. Student-only tutor de bi so sanh voi chatbot generic; teacher-controlled workflow co kha nang tao trust va school distribution tot hon.

Ket luan: idea nay **fundable co dieu kien**. Investor nhu EduSpaze, ThinkZone, Antler, 500 Global SEA, hoac FEBE co the quan tam neu pitch khong chi noi "AI tutor", ma chung minh duoc wedge cu the trong school workflow Viet Nam/SEA. Bang chung quan trong nhat can co la: usage retention, willingness to pay, AI quality/safety, va unit economics duong. Neu khong co cac bang chung do, idea de bi xem la mot AI feature de copy, chua phai venture-backable company.
