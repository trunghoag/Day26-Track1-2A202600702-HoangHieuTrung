## 3. Investor-by-Investor Notes

### EduSpaze

- **Investment thesis:** Edtech startups phuc vu Singapore va Southeast Asia; ho tro go-to-market, co-innovation, partner/school connection.
- **Same-space evidence:** Ban than EduSpaze la edtech accelerator, nen khong can ep thesis qua sector khac.
- **Typical ticket size:** S$100K-S$500K; up to S$500K through convertible note.
- **Investment criteria:** Seed to pre-Series A; product ready, co traction va sales revenue; co quan tam SEA/APAC.
- **Why yes:** Idea AI tutor/teacher assistant can dung nhat cai EduSpaze co: school access, pilot, va education mentors.
- **Why no:** Neu product moi chi demo ky thuat, chua co giao vien/truong nao test, EduSpaze co the thay chua du customer discovery.

### 500 Global Southeast Asia

- **Investment thesis:** Back technology founders based in or serving Southeast Asia, with regional scale potential.
- **Same-space evidence:** 500 Global SEA la generalist early-stage investor; site cua ho co nhac den ed-tech trong nhom Human & Machine Productivity.
- **Typical ticket size:** US$250K-US$500K for early-stage SEA funds.
- **Investment criteria:** Founder ambition, regional market, growth potential, ability to scale beyond one small pilot.
- **Why yes:** Neu MVP co user retention va plan mo rong sang SEA, 500 Global co network va capital khop pre-seed/seed.
- **Why no:** Neu idea chi la tool lop hoc nho, khong co go-to-market scalable, co the khong du venture-scale.

### Antler SEA

- **Investment thesis:** Pre-seed, verticalized AI, industry-specific startups, hyperlocal SEA solutions with global scalability.
- **Same-space evidence:** Antler cong bo round US$5.1M cho 37 SEA startups, bao gom AI va B2B SaaS; ho nhan manh verticalized AI.
- **Typical ticket size:** Khong nen xem US$138K la standard ticket chinh thuc; day chi la benchmark suy ra tu tong funding cong bo chia cho so startup.
- **Investment criteria:** Founder quality, very early-stage conviction, pain point lon, kha nang tao business model ben vung.
- **Why yes:** Phu hop neu team dang pre-seed/prototype va can support tu luc xay dung foundation.
- **Why no:** Neu team muon capital lon hon ngay hoac da co traction seed-level, Antler co the khong phai lead investor phu hop nhat.

### ThinkZone Ventures

- **Investment thesis:** Seed to Series A tech startups, diverse verticals, uu tien Vietnam market hoac co Vietnamese founder; danh gia team, market, traction.
- **Same-space evidence:** ThinkZone noi portfolio co fintech, edtech, transportation, e-commerce.
- **Typical ticket size:** Up to US$3M cho Seed to Series A; accelerator-stage US$50K-US$100K.
- **Investment criteria:** Team co expertise, market size/growth du lon, USP/advantage, traction nhu MAU/GMV/partners va growth rate.
- **Why yes:** Fit neu idea can local Vietnam distribution, corporate/school partnerships, va investor hieu thi truong Viet Nam.
- **Why no:** Neu chua co Vietnamese founder/operation tai Vietnam hoac chua co traction, can vao accelerator track truoc thay vi VC check lon.

### FEBE Ventures

- **Investment thesis:** Seed-focused VC gan voi Vietnam/SEA; Fund II US$75M, focus pre-seed/seed companies across sectors and geographies.
- **Same-space evidence:** FEBE co focus Vietnam va seed-stage tech, nen phu hop neu Edtech AI MVP co growth evidence.
- **Typical ticket size:** Khong thay public individual check size dang tin cay; vi vay chi ghi fund size US$75M va stage focus pre-seed/seed.
- **Investment criteria:** Seed-stage startup co founder quality, scalable tech product, market potential, va path to follow-on round.
- **Why yes:** Fit khi product da co pilot, early paying users, va co cau chuyen mo rong SEA.
- **Why no:** Neu moi o pre-idea/prototype chua co retention, FEBE co the doi them evidence truoc khi dau tu.
